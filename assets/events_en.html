AL101 - Active Learning Workshop
Asticou Centre

AL101 - Active Learning Workshop

Title AL101 - Active Learning Workshop
Date 2016-02-16
Time 9:00 am - 4:00 pm
Location Asticou Centre
Description

The Classroom 2020 team (formerly known as Learning Space Modernization) invites you to a workshop dedicated to active learning. This workshop is the first step towards transforming the classroom experience at the School. 

Where does the classroom sit in the School’s new Learning Ecosystem? 

Why come to class?

In order to align with 21st century classroom renewal efforts, we must make sure that the time learners invest attending courses at the School maximizes the potential of the participants and provides direct positive  impact for the sponsoring organizations.

How do we manage this? Part of the answer is that we use active learning!

Principle: In this workshop, you will be completely immersed in an active learning environment; you will experience and explore multiple dimensions of this approach.

Length:  6 hours

Who: instructional designers, facilitators, faculty (priority for these three groups). Any other School employee who is curious or is interested in training can also attend.

IMPORTANT NOTE: This is NOT a workshop on technology. The workshop is an overview and a great introduction to active learning in a classroom environment.

For this workshop to be successful, we require a minimum number of participants: 24 spots are available for each session

Share the news with your colleagues, bring a buddy, or come with your entire team!

We will send you the logistics details later on.

This workshop on active learning will be followed by more specific and shorter workshops. It will also be offered again during the year. Keep an eye open!

This workshop will only be offered in-person (no virtual attendance). Please contact Annie Leblond or Doug Brubacher if you would like to speak with us about having the workshop delivered in your region.  

 
doug.brubacher@canada.ca doug.brubacher@canada.ca
